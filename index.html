Natural Attractions

Blue Eye (Syri i Kaltër)

A stunning natural spring with crystal-clear blue water, located near Sarandë. This magical water source is over 50 meters deep and maintains a constant temperature.

Natural Spring Sarandë
Llogara Pass

Breathtaking mountain pass offering panoramic views of the Albanian Riviera and Ionian Sea. Perfect for hiking and photography enthusiasts.

Mountain Pass Vlorë
Theth National Park

A pristine alpine park in the Albanian Alps featuring waterfalls, traditional stone houses, and excellent hiking trails through untouched wilderness.

National Park Shkodër
Karaburun Peninsula

Untouched coastal wilderness featuring dramatic cliffs, hidden beaches, and marine protected areas. Accessible only by boat or hiking.

Marine Park Vlorë
Lake Ohrid

One of Europe's oldest and deepest lakes, shared with North Macedonia. A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its endemic species and crystal-clear waters.

UNESCO Site Pogradec
Divjakë-Karavasta National Park

Coastal wetland paradise home to the largest lagoon in Albania and the endangered Dalmatian pelican. Perfect for birdwatching and nature photography.

Wetland Park Fier

Cultural Museums

National History Museum

Albania's largest museum showcasing the country's history from prehistoric times to the present. Features the famous mosaic "The Albanians" on its facade.

History Tirana
BunkArt Museum

Unique museum housed in a former communist-era bunker, offering insights into Albania's totalitarian past through contemporary art and historical exhibitions.

Contemporary Art Tirana
Marubi National Museum

The first photography museum in the Balkans, housing over 500,000 negatives documenting Albanian life from the 1850s onwards.

Photography Shkodër
Onufri Museum

Located inside Berat Castle, this museum showcases the works of Onufri, a renowned 16th-century Albanian icon painter, and other religious art.

Religious Art Berat
Ethnographic Museum

Housed in the birthplace of Enver Hoxha, this museum displays traditional Albanian folk costumes, crafts, and cultural artifacts from different regions.

Folk Culture Gjirokastër
Archaeological Museum

Features artifacts from ancient Illyrian, Greek, and Roman periods found throughout Albania, including beautiful mosaics and sculptures.

Archaeology Durrës
